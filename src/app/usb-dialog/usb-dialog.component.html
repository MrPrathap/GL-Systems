USB

USB 3.2 Gen 1

Arena 1 has 4 individual USB 3.2 ports located in rear side, which has 5 Gbps data transfer speed. So data transfer made faster.

Both Type A and Type C interfaces supported.

USB 2.0

Arena 1 has 2 individual USB 2.0 ports located in rear side, offers a transfer rate of about 480 Mbps.
